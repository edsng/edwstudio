Business Brief: Auto Detailing Demo

Business name:
Diamond Touch Detailing

Location:
Ontario, California

Business type:
Premium auto detailing business

Business summary:
A high-end detailing company specializing in ceramic coating, paint correction, interior detailing, maintenance washes, and premium exterior protection. The brand should feel glossy, modern, premium, and automotive enthusiast-focused. This business wants to attract owners who truly care about the condition and appearance of their vehicles.

Target audience:
- Car enthusiasts
- Luxury vehicle owners
- Tesla owners
- Daily drivers wanting premium care
- Customers interested in ceramic coating and paint correction

Main conversion goal:
Generate quote requests and service bookings

Sitemap:
- Home
- Services
- Gallery
- Packages
- Reviews
- FAQ
- Contact / Quote Request

Homepage structure:
1. Hero section with premium automotive imagery and clear CTA
2. Trust bar with service specialties and review highlights
3. Featured services section
4. Before-and-after transformation gallery
5. Why choose us section
6. Packages / pricing preview
7. Testimonial section
8. FAQ section
9. Quote request CTA band
10. Footer with service area and contact info

Key UI components:
- Sticky mobile CTA button
- Before/after image slider
- Service cards
- Quote request form
- Testimonial cards
- FAQ accordion
- Service area map/list
- Gallery grid

Visual style:
Dark, sleek, glossy, premium, modern, automotive-inspired. Strong contrast, bold typography, high-end spacing, polished UI.

Image plan by section:
- Hero: clean luxury or enthusiast car close-up with glossy finish
- Services: detailing process shots
- Gallery: before/after exterior and interior transformations
- Testimonials: abstract automotive or customer-vehicle visuals instead of obvious faces
- CTA sections: premium car in natural light or garage environment

Example CTAs:
- Get a Free Quote
- Book Your Detail
- See Our Work
- Protect Your Paint
- Schedule Your Service

What makes this demo strong in a portfolio:
The gallery and before/after presentation should be one of the strongest visual parts of the site. It should feel like a business someone would trust with an expensive car.
